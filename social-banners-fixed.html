📥 How to Save & Use These Banners

For LinkedIn:

  1. Right-click on the LinkedIn banner above
  2. Select "Save Image As..."
  3. Save as PNG or JPG to your computer
  4. Go to your LinkedIn profile → Click the camera icon on your header → Upload the image

For YouTube:

  1. Right-click on the YouTube banner above
  2. Select "Save Image As..."
  3. Save as PNG or JPG to your computer
  4. Go to YouTube Studio → Customization → Branding → Upload banner
